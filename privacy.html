Privacy notice

Last updated: June 2026.

This is an informational website for Elephant Hawk LLC. It is intentionally simple, and it is designed to collect as little information as possible.

What we collect

This site has no accounts and no tracking or advertising cookies. Our contact form stores nothing on this site — it simply opens a pre-filled message in your own email app, which you choose to send. If you contact us (directly or via the form), we receive whatever you send so that we can reply.

Third-party services

The site loads typefaces from Google Fonts. When your browser requests those fonts, Google may receive your IP address and basic request data, as described in Google's own privacy policy. We use no other third-party trackers or analytics.

Hosting logs

Our web host keeps standard server logs (for example, IP addresses and pages requested) for security and operations. These are managed by the host as part of providing the service.

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@elephanthawk.com. General inquiries: office@elephanthawk.com.
