The process · Chrysalis

Every engagement runs on one engine: Chrysalis.

Chrysalis isn't a product you buy on its own — it's how the work runs. An AI-native process that carries a regulated medtech product from concept to supply, compressing a timeline that usually takes years. The documentation that consumes most of a regulated calendar is AI-drafted and engineer-owned, wired to FDA and clinical databases, and quality-gated at every step.

The building blocks · SEMI

Every product composes from four reusable layers.

Chrysalis is a layer-composition engine. Rather than rebuild each device from scratch, products are composed from four libraries — Sensing, Energy, Motion, Intelligence. They grow with every cleared product, they compound into a moat, and they stay internal to Elephant Hawk.

S

Sensing

Sensors · transducers · imaging · biochemistry · electrochemistry.

E

Energy

Power and therapeutic delivery — electrical · acoustic · thermal · optical · magnetic · pressure.

M

I

Intelligence

Algorithms · AI/ML · signal processing · firmware · edge inference.
