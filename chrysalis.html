AI-infused. Full lifecycle. Every phase of design, fully leveraged.

Chrysalis is the engine inside Elephant Hawk — an AI-native platform that carries a regulated medtech product from concept to supply, compressing the timeline that usually takes years. The documentation that consumes most of a regulated calendar is AI-drafted and engineer-owned.

Not another services firm. An engine.

Most regulated timelines are lost in documentation and handoffs. Chrysalis attacks exactly that.
