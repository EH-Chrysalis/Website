Overview

The structure inside which transformation happens.

The path from a breakthrough idea to a product in the hands of the people who need it is too slow, too fragmented, and too expensive. Elephant Hawk exists to compress that path.

Why we exist

Life is delicate. The work is to preserve it, enable it, and advance it — every life, anywhere life exists.

Preserve · Enable · Advance

Our vision

Elephant Hawk is building a compounding value ecosystem where anyone with a healthcare insight, technology, capability, or capital can convert it into regulated medtech impact — globally, on working unit economics, at a cycle time the conventional industry cannot match.

The problem

Healthcare progress is bottlenecked by execution, not by ideas. Great work stalls in the gap between invention and the market — regulatory complexity, manufacturing barriers, and fragmented expertise turn a clear breakthrough into a long, expensive, uncertain road.

That gap doesn't just slow companies down. It keeps products from the people who need them.

What we hold

Elephant Hawk partners with industry and innovators to develop, manufacture, and bring regulated medtech products to market worldwide.

It's more than the idea. We supply the full operational backbone — market and regulatory analysis, design and engineering, feasibility, supply-chain and IP, clinical evidence and quality — all distilled through Chrysalis into a product that clears, so a promising idea becomes a product on the market faster and with less risk. The partner gets the credit; we hold the conditions.

The structure

One engine, two companies.

Elephant Hawk is the engine in the United States — far more than a commercial front door. Market and regulatory analysis, design, feasibility, IP, clinical and quality work, commercialization and capital all run through Elephant Hawk, on Chrysalis.

IF MedTech is the manufacturing and build engine — an ISO 13485 medical-device maker that turns a cleared design into product at scale.

The two companies share a founder — Dr. Rupesh Ghyar — and work in parallel. Elephant Hawk runs the engine; IF MedTech builds at scale.

Ideas crawl in. Products fly out.

Meet the founder

Dr. Rupesh Ghyar

Founder & CEO, Elephant Hawk

  • PhD · Biomechanical engineering
  • Director, BETIC
  • IIT Bombay

Dr. Rupesh Ghyar is a biomechanical engineer who has spent his career turning medical-device research into products that reach patients. He directed the Medtech Lab (BETIC) at IIT Bombay — one of India's foremost medical-device innovation centres — carrying device programs from concept through regulatory clearance and manufacture. He purpose-built Elephant Hawk's engine, Chrysalis, to do that work faster and more reliably, for industry and innovators alike. He works between the US and India, and still answers his own email and WhatsApp.
